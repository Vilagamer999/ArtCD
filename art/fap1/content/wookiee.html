About Wookiee:

I have been drawing for as long as I can remember. My work consisted mostly of wildlife pencils, horse portraits in oils and some cartoon work. In 1995 I was introduced to the Furry Fandom and since then have been drawing mainly furry art ever since. I am a Canadian fur just about to turn 40. My alias, 'Wookiee', was given to me by my friends long before I knew what a furry was since I tend to be a very hairy fellow. Here are a few samples of my work.
